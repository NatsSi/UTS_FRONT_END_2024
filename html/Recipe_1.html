Avocado Toast with Eggs

A breakfast recipe with low glycemic index, for sinking the craving of 10-11am! Can be conveniently prepared the day before.

Moderate

Newbie

10 Minutes Preparation

20 Minutes Cooking

841 Kcal Per Serving

Ingredients

1 ripe avocado
2 large eggs
2 slices of whole bread
1 teaspoon lemon juice
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crisp.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl and mix in the lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. In a pan, cook the eggs as desired (scrambled, poached, or fried).
  4. Spread the mashed avocado mixture over the toast slices.
  5. Top with the cooked eggs and season with more salt and pepper if needed.
